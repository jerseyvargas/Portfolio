Campbell Scholars Program

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, After Effects, Indesign

Projects: Motion Graphics, Video, Yearbook, Nametags, Documents, Calendar



The Campbell Scholars Program is a tuition-free academic enrichment and college access program designed to support low-income, high-achieving public school students aspiring to be the first in their family to graduate from a four-year college.


For the duration of the summer of 2024, I was in charge of developing graphic and motion design material, along with helping direct photography for the program's use.


I was later commissioned again from June-July 2025 for their yearbook, name tags, and digital graphics (bus route document, calendar).
