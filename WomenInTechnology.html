Women In Technology Conference

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, After Effects

Role: Lead Designer

Projects: Social Media Graphics, Sponsorship Packet, Promotional Material, Web Design, Newsletter Template, Motion Graphics



Tufts Women in Technology (WiT) is a student organization that hosts annual tech conferences celebrating women and nonbinary individuals in technology. During the summer of 2024, I worked within the WiT Design & PR Committee to develop promotional content in preparation for their event. This year's graphics followed a theme of retrofuturism.

All design work displayed are solo projects assigned and carried out by me, with the exception of the crown illustrations on the Sponsor Tier Sheet as well as the 2024 WiT Logo.

CONFERENCE FLYERS

MY IMPACT: Sponsorship Increase

This annual conference aims to exceed the fundraising achievements of previous years. One of my initial tasks was to redesign the Sponsor Tier Sheet so that it may better reflect sponsorship deals and set a precedent for the rest of our designs.

Within half a month of this new design being released, $8K in funds were reached, seeing a 15% increase in sponsorship between the months of May to June as compared to the year prior.
